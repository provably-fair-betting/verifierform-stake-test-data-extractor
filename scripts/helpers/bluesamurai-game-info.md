1. All symbols pay left to right. Winnings are based on Pay Table.

2. Player plays 40 lines on every spin. All wins are multiplied by bet per line, with bonus rounds and special multiplied by total bet.

3. Wild symbols substitute every symbol except Scatter. In the case of multiple wild symbols in a winning pay line, highest multiplier will be paid only. If multipliers are equal, only one prize will be paid. Wild does not affect Special game.

4. Scatters can only appear on 2nd, 3rd and 4th reel.

5. Three or more Scatters will give you 10 bonus round spins. Bonus round prizes pay extra.

6. Bonus rounds are capped at 180 free spins.

7. If the 1st or 5th reel is spun with three Samurais, you will get access to the Special Game.

8. During the Special Game, you have five chances to complete the board with ONLY Samurais. The number of Samurais spun will pay accordingly with the Special Game Pay Table. There are no extra spins awarded in this round.
